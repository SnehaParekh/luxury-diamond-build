About

A Computer Engineer by profession, Ankita Jain decided to leave behind her static 9-6 desk job to fuel her passion for jewelry craftsmanship. Having gained expertise in diamond grading and valuation, Luxury Diamonds is her venture to craft jewellery which are a unique blend of both classic and contemporary.

Her motto:

“No inflated prices or middlemen. Just a unique selection of authentic diamond jewellery at a fair price.”
